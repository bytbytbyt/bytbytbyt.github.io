<h2 id="about-me"><i class="fas fa-user section-icon" aria-hidden="true"></i>About Me</h2>

<p>I am currently a master's student in <strong>Electronics Science and Technology</strong> at the <strong>Department of Mechanical and Energy Engineering</strong>, <strong>Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)</strong>, where I work in <strong>StarLab</strong> under the supervision of <a href="https://robotics-star.com/people"><strong>Prof. Boyu Zhou</strong></a>. I received my <strong>B.Eng. in Information Engineering</strong> from the <strong>Department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering</strong> at SUSTech in 2025.</p>

<p>My research and engineering interests focus on <strong>UAV visual-language navigation, embodied perception, robot vision systems, and efficient onboard deployment</strong>.</p>

<p>During my undergraduate years, I served in the visual group of the <strong>ARTINX RoboMaster Team</strong>, first as a member and later as a leader, where I worked on energy mechanism targeting, engineering vision, auto-aiming, and model optimization for multiple robot platforms.</p>
